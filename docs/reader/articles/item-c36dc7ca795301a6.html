Recommended resources: Explore issues at the core of WSIS 2026

The World Summit on the Information Society (WSIS) Forum 2026 will take place from 5 to 9 July 2026 in Geneva, Switzerland. This hybrid event is hosted by the International Telecommunication Union (ITU) and Switzerland. APC staff, members and partners will once again use this important event to amplify the voices of our network, exploring emerging issues and digital priorities (find out more about APC's participation here). To prepare for the upcoming forum, we recommend the following resources for background on APC’s engagement at WSIS, as well as context on some of the key issues we are focusing on.
